# ApplyWise: AI-Powered Job Application Automation

ApplyWise is a modular, AI-driven system that automates your entire job application process-from discovering opportunities to sending personalized emails with your resume. Built with n8n workflows, DeepSeek, Perplexity, and Gmail integration, it’s your smart assistant for landing the perfect job.

---

## Features

-  **Smart Job Discovery**  
  Pulls job listings from LinkedIn, RSS feeds and filters duplicates.

-  **AI-Powered Company Research**  
  Uses DeepSeek and Perplexity to extract company summaries and contact emails.

-  **Personalized Cover Letters**  
  Generates tailored letters using your resume and company-specific details.

-  **Resume Integration**  
  Automatically attaches your resume from Google Drive.

-  **Automated Email Delivery**  
  Sends personalized applications via Gmail with tracking confirmation.

-  **Chat-Based Control**  
  Trigger workflows using natural language commands.

-  **Modular Workflow Design**  
  Three separate workflows for discovery, personalization, and delivery.

---

##  Technologies Used

- n8n – Workflow automation
- DeepSeek – AI-powered search
- Perplexity – Company info and contact discovery
- Google Sheets– Job tracking
- Gmail API – Email delivery
- Python – Resume parsing and email formatting
